# 🍕 Pizza Sales Analysis Dashboard (SQL + Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

Developed an interactive **Pizza Sales Dashboard** using **SQL** and **Power BI** to analyze one year of sales data. Performed SQL-based business analysis to calculate key performance metrics and built an interactive dashboard to visualize revenue, sales trends, product performance, and customer ordering patterns, enabling data-driven business decisions.

## 📂 Data Source

- Publicly available Pizza Sales Dataset sourced from Kaggle.

## 📜 SQL Analysis

The following SQL queries were used to analyze the dataset:

- Total Revenue
- Average Order Value
- Total Orders
- Total Pizzas Sold
- Average Pizzas per Order
- Daily Order Trend
- Monthly Order Trend
- Sales by Pizza Category
- Sales by Pizza Size
- Top 5 & Bottom 5 Pizzas by Revenue
- Top 5 & Bottom 5 Pizzas by Quantity
- Top 5 & Bottom 5 Pizzas by Total Orders

## 📈 Business Insights

- Weekend evenings (Friday & Saturday) recorded the highest order volume.
- July generated the highest number of customer orders.
- Classic Pizza category contributed the highest revenue and total sales.
- Large-sized pizzas accounted for the highest share of sales.
- The Thai Chicken Pizza generated the highest revenue.
- The Brie Carre Pizza ranked among the lowest-performing products.

## 💡 Business Impact

- Identified top-performing and low-performing pizza products.
- Enabled sales performance monitoring through interactive KPI dashboards.
- Supported inventory and menu optimization using product-level insights.
- Simplified business reporting with dynamic visualizations and filters.
- Enabled faster, data-driven decision-making for stakeholders.
